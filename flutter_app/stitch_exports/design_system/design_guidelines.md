# Obsidian Flux Design Notes

- Creative north star: "The Observational Command"
- Avoid hard divider lines; use tonal surface differences and spacing
- Dark editorial aesthetic with high data density and premium image visibility
- Primary CTA treatment favors subtle primary-to-primary-container gradients
- Preferred text color is soft (`on_surface`/`on_background`), not pure white
- Keep hierarchy by nesting surface layers instead of heavy borders
